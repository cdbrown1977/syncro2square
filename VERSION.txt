Syncro to Square Invoice Sync
Version 1.0.0
Release Date: February 5, 2026

Tested and working with:
- WAMP Server 3.4.0
- Apache 2.4.65
- PHP 8.3.28
- MySQL 8.4.7 / MariaDB 11.4.9

Features:
- Automatic invoice syncing from Syncro MSP to Square
- Customer lookup by email (prevents duplicates)
- Multiple line items support
- Draft invoice creation
- Comprehensive logging
- Duplicate prevention
